# TaiLueNMT_Dataset
Private parallel corpus for Tai Lue-Chinese neural machine translation, collected from Pattra palm-leaf manuscripts and manually proofread by native linguistic consultants.
==================================================== 
Dataset Acquisition and Usage Instructions (ReadMe)
==================================================== 

Welcome to use this dataset! [cite: 1] This dataset is provided free of charge for academic research. To protect data intellectual property rights and standardize the usage process, the dataset compressed package (.rar) has been protected with a decompression password. 

Please follow the steps below to obtain the decompression password: 

1. Fill out the Letter of Commitment: 
   Please open and fill out the accompanying "Dataset Usage Application and Letter of Commitment.docx".

2. Carefully read and confirm the following usage conditions: 
   - It must absolutely not be used for any commercial purposes; it is strictly limited to academic research. 
   - In any academic achievements generated using this dataset, you must properly cite our relevant paper (see "Citation Format" below for paper details). 
   - You must leave your contact email address in the Letter of Commitment. 

3. Stamp and send the email: 
   Please print the completed Letter of Commitment and affix your institution/team's official seal. 
   Send a scanned copy (or high-definition photo) of the stamped Letter of Commitment to our email: [286678414@qq.com] 

4. Obtain the password:
   After we receive your application email and verify its contents, we will send the decompression password directly to the email address you provided in the Letter of Commitment.
